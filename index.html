Frequently asked

No. MoneyMoves AU is a general educational tool. It is not personal financial advice and is not a substitute for a licensed Australian financial adviser. Use the output as a starting point for your own thinking or a conversation with a professional.
Do I need to give you my bank details?
Never. The tool only asks for the numbers you'd punch into a calculator — balances, rates, monthly income. No logins, no Open Banking, no data scraping.
Is my information private?
Yes. We don't store your financial inputs. Email is only used to notify you about the launch and major updates — unsubscribe any time.

Yes — the free tool is live right now. Click "Try it free" at the top of the page. No waitlist, no login, no bank details required.
