Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What we collect

MoneyMoves AU collects only what you enter into the tool:

  • Financial inputs (income, expenses, savings, debt balances and rates) — used only to generate your plan
  • Email address — only if you choose to provide it to receive your plan or PDF

We do not collect your name, Tax File Number, bank account details, or any identifying information.

How we use your data

  • Your financial inputs are processed in your browser and on our servers solely to generate your personalised output
  • We do not store your financial inputs after your session ends
  • If you purchase the PDF, your email is used to deliver it and for no other purpose unless you opt in to updates

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate MoneyMoves AU:

  • PayPal — processes payments. Your payment details are handled entirely by PayPal and are not visible to us.
  • Vercel — hosts this application. Subject to Vercel's privacy policy.
  • Resend — delivers email. Your email address is passed to Resend solely to send your PDF.

Cookies and analytics

MoneyMoves AU does not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking. We may use anonymous visitor counts to understand usage volume.

Your rights

You may request deletion of any data we hold about you by emailing us. Since we do not store financial inputs, deletion requests typically apply to email addresses only.

Contact

For any privacy questions, contact us at privacy@moneymoves.com.au.

Disclaimer

MoneyMoves AU provides general financial information only. It is not personal financial advice and does not consider your individual circumstances. Before acting on any output, consider whether it is appropriate for you and consult a licensed Australian financial adviser.
